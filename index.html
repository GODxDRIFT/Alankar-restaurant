Our Story

Born in Delhi,
Made with Love


Alankar Restaurant is a beloved Pure Veg destination in the heart of Uttam Nagar, New Delhi. What started as a dream to serve authentic North Indian vegetarian cuisine has grown into a community institution where families gather, memories are made, and every meal feels like home.

We've never compromised on our core promise — 100% Pure Vegetarian, always. From our smoky tandoor to our slow-cooked dals, every dish honours the rich culinary traditions of Northern India. No shortcuts. No artificial flavours. Just real ingredients, real recipes, and real love.

Whether you're stopping by for a quick lunch thali or celebrating a family occasion over dinner, we treat every guest like family. Come taste the difference that shudh shakahari cooking makes.
